Home Privacy Policy
This privacy policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and provides information about your rights and choices. 1. Introduction
1.1
Welcome to the privacy policy of SwyChat (the “App”). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the App. 2. Information We Collect
2.1
We may collect personal information that you provide when creating an account with or using the App, including but not limited to: User account information (e.g., username, email address, phone number). Profile information (e.g., name, profile picture). Communication content (e.g., messages, calls, video conferences). Payment information (if applicable). 2.2
We may collect certain information automatically when you use the App, including: Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, unique device identifiers). Usage data (e.g., app activity, access times). IP address. 2.3
The App utilizes blockchain technology for security and data verification. This may involve collecting and storing blockchain transaction data and chat history associated with your account. 3. How We Use Your Information
3.1
We use your personal information to provide, maintain, and improve the App’s functionality, including chat, audio, and video call services. 3.2
We may use your information to send you updates, notifications, and important service-related messages. 3.3
We may use your information to personalize your experience and provide content, features, and service updates that are relevant to you. 3.4
We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, respond to requests from law enforcement, or protect our rights and the rights of others. 4. Data Security
4.1
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. 4.2
Your blockchain keys and passwords are crucial for the security of your data. We do not have access to your keys, and you are solely responsible for their safekeeping. 5. Data Sharing
5.1
We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing and improving the App. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements. 5.2
We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, respond to legal requests, or protect our rights and the rights of others. 6. Your Choices
6.1
You can review and update your account information through the App settings. 6.2
You can manage your communication preferences by adjusting notification settings. 7. Children’s Privacy
7.1
The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. 8. Changes to Privacy Policy
8.1
We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of any significant changes through the App or other means. 9. Contact Information
9.1
If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at
info@swychat.io
