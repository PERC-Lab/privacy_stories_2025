Non-personal data In this Privacy Policy, "non-personal data" refers to information that does not directly identify you. Examples of non-personal data collected and used by Mobprem include application properties such as the application name, package name, and icon installed on your device. Your check-ins (including likes and recommendations) for a game will be shared with all Mobprem users. Mobprem may utilize and disclose the collected non-personal data to partners and contractors for purposes such as analyzing usage of the Services, serving advertisements, managing and providing the Services, and further developing Mobprem's services and products. You acknowledge and agree that the analytics companies used by Mobprem may combine the collected information with other data they have independently obtained from other services or products related to your activities. These companies collect and handle information according to their own privacy policies. Personal Data As defined in this Privacy Policy, "personal data" refers to personally identifiable information that specifically identifies you as an individual. Mobprem collects personal information voluntarily provided by you when creating or updating your account. This information includes your Facebook ID, name, gender, location, and your friends' IDs on Facebook. Additionally, Mobprem stores data such as your game check-ins, likes, dislikes, recommendations, and messages. Collected personal data may be used by Mobprem for purposes such as analyzing Service usage, providing customer and technical support, managing and delivering Services (including advertising), and further developing Mobprem's services and products. Mobprem may also combine non-personal data with personal data. Please note that certain features of the Services may connect to your social networking sites to gather additional information about you. In such cases, Mobprem may collect specific information from your social networking profile with your consent, including your name, profile picture, gender, user ID, email address, country, language, time zone, organizations and links on your profile page, and the names and profile pictures of your social networking "friends." Mobprem may associate, combine, and use this information collected from social networking sites in accordance with this Privacy Policy. Disclosure and Transfer of Personal Data Mobprem collects and processes personal data on a voluntary basis and does not sell personal data to third parties. However, personal data may be disclosed in accordance with applicable laws and this Privacy Policy. Mobprem may also disclose personal data to its parent companies and subsidiaries as outlined in this Privacy Policy. Mobprem may engage agents and contractors to collect and process personal data on its behalf. In such cases, these third parties are instructed to comply with Mobprem's Privacy Policy and use personal data solely for the intended purposes. These agents and contractors are prohibited from using your personal data for their own marketing purposes. Mobprem may also utilize third-party service providers (e.g., credit card processors, email service providers, shipping agents, data analyzers, and business intelligence providers) who need access to your personal data to provide their services. Mobprem is not responsible for the actions or omissions of these third parties, except as required by law. Your personal data may be disclosed to third parties if required by law enforcement or other government officials in cases involving fraud, intellectual property infringement, or illegal activity that may jeopardize Mobprem or yourself. Additionally, personal data may be disclosed if Mobprem believes it is necessary to protect its rights, combat fraud, comply with legal proceedings, court orders, or other legal processes. Mobprem will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such disclosures, as permitted by applicable law, through its website or other reasonable means. Safeguards Mobprem adheres to industry-standard practices and maintains reasonable safeguards to protect the security, integrity, and privacy of the information it possesses. Only authorized personnel involved in fulfilling their tasks in accordance with the purposes of this Privacy Policy and for technical maintenance have access to your personal data. Personal data collected by Mobprem is stored in secure environments inaccessible to the public. Mobprem employs firewalls to protect personal data from unauthorized online access. However, no system can provide 100% security, and despite Mobprem's efforts, there remains a possibility of unauthorized access to personal data. By using the Services, you acknowledge and assume this risk. Other Please be mindful of the open nature of certain social networking and other features provided by Mobprem in its Services.
