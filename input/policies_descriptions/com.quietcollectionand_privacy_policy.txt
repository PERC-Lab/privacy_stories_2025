Privacy Policy
We recognize that privacy of your personal information is important. Here is information on what types of personal information we receive and collect when you use and visit
emilypfreeman.com
and how we safeguard your information.
We never sell your personal information to third parties.
Here at  emilypfreeman.com , we process personal, relevant data from website visitors and subscribers. This data is collected for the purpose of providing services and/or content, as well as to compliment the experience of those visiting
emilypfreeman.com .
We retain the account data that you voluntarily provided to us (your name and email address) as long as you have an account (ex:  The Quiet Collection  app). If you no longer wish to have your account, you may delete it at anytime.
If you no longer wish to receive email from me, you can simply unsubscribe. This link is found at the bottom of my emails. Other ways data may be collected and stored include: analytics & traffic logs.
Any user information collected from subscribers and/or visitors is NEVER sold or shared by me.
