Privacy Policy for khm.app Progressive Web App (PWA)
Effective Date: [Insert Effective Date]
Thank you for using khm.app Progressive Web App (PWA). This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our PWA. By using khm.app PWA, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
1. Information We Collect
1.1. Personal Information We may collect the following personal information from you when you use khm.app PWA:
- Contact Information: This may include your name, email address, and phone number, which you provide voluntarily when contacting us or using certain features of the PWA. - Usage Data: We collect information about your interaction with our PWA, including pages visited, actions taken, and error messages, to improve our services and enhance your experience.
1.2. Automatically Collected Information We may also collect certain information automatically when you use khm.app PWA, such as:
- Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our PWA, including device type, operating system, and browser type. - Log Data: We automatically log information about your use of the PWA, such as IP address, access times, and pages viewed.
1.3. Cookies and Similar Technologies We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior and preferences. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain the PWA. - To improve and personalize your experience. - To analyze usage patterns and optimize the PWA. - To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support. - To send you updates, newsletters, and marketing communications, where you have opted in to receive them. - To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security concerns.
3. Data Sharing
We may share your personal information with third parties in the following circumstances:
- Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate, maintain, and improve the PWA. - Analytics: We use Google Analytics and Hotjar to analyze user behavior and optimize the PWA. These third-party services may collect and process data as described in their respective privacy policies. - Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law, in response to legal requests, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
4. Your Choices
- Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in such communications. - Cookies: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
5. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
7. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information, please get in touch with us at:
info@khm.app
By using the.app PWA, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms and conditions.
