Privacy Policy We recognize that privacy is Rajesh App Studio. We have created this Policy to explain our privacy practices so you can understand what information about you is collected, used and disclosed. We collect information from you in order to provide corresponding service and better user experience. With your consent of this Privacy Policy, your usage, statistics, input while using our apps would be collected. HOW WE USE COLLECTED INFORMATION We may use your information only for serving you better as below: To personalize the user experience. If user grant the permission that means user provide access to his/her personal information. We stores that data only on his/her device.
We don’t store their personal or non-personal information anywhere else. To help develop our service - We may use Non-Personal Information to provide, maintain, improve and develop our services. We may collect your non-personal information and use them for our market and promotion purpose, for example, we may promote or recommend more relevant apps to you. Device Permissions for Personal Data access: Phone permission, Contacts permission, Camera permission, and Storage permission Device Permissions: Camera Permission: Access the camera for capture image. Contact Permission: Access to get contacts id. Through contacts id, one can set Contact image URI(Uniform Resource Identifier) and set Ringtone audio URI Directly from application. Storage Permission: Access Storage for read and write files. Phone Permission: Access Phone permission to use normal features like link with telephony services. For example, get Mobile Number, "Phone state" status of the calls and current mobile network information. We do not store any information using phone permission. SMS Permission: Access sms permission for messaging operations like read, receive and send text messages. It can be for backup & restore use, too. We use this information to operate, maintain, and provide to you the features and functionality of our Apps, as well as to communicate directly with you, such as to send you to email messages and push notifications. We may also send you Service-related emails or messages (e.g., account verification, order confirmations, change or updates to features of our apps, technical and security notices). HOW YOUR INFORMATION MAY BE DISCLOSED Personal Information. We do not store Personal Information and therefore we do not disclose your Personal Information. Non-Personal Information. We do not combine Non-Personal Information with Personal Information (such as combining your name with your unique User Device number). We did not disclose your non-personal information for promotion purpose. If we store personal information, that info retain at a time without disclose your Personal Information. We only read phone state and set our functionality to give user then retain clear all private information from app. PERSONAL DATA CONCERNING CHILDREN we does not knowingly collect any personal data concerning children under 15 years old.
WHAT DATA IS COLLECTED ? With your prior consent, we are likely to collect and process the following data in whole or in part when you use our applications. Identity-related data: for some of our applications, we are likely to collect data from your Facebook account, when you  login  using Facebook connect: Public profile (surname, forename, age, gender, email address, profile photos, hobbies), friend list IDFA publicity identifiers (for IOS devices) and GAID (for Android devices) Data pertaining to your activities on our applications and notably the way in which you interact with our applications (for instance, how and when you use our applications) and with the publicities we display (for instance, time spent on these, potential clicks) Data pertaining to your geographical position (IP address) We are also likely to use cookies which correspond to small files containing letters and figures downloaded onto your device when you access our applications.
