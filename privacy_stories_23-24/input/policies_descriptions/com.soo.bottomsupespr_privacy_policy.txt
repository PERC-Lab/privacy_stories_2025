Privacy Policy of Zaytech Corp
Owner and Data Controller
Zaytech Corp
contact email: support@zaytech.com
Types of Data collected:
At ZayTech, we employ various data capture tools such as Meta Pixel, Google Tags, and Microsoft Clarity.
Why do we collect data?
The purpose of data collection for our company is to gather relevant information and insights that enable us to better understand our customers, improve our products and services, and enhance their overall experience. By collecting data, we aim to:
Personalize and enhance customer experiences: Data helps us tailor our offerings to individual preferences, allowing us to deliver personalized recommendations, offers, and services that align with customer needs and interests.
Improve products and services: Data analysis provides valuable feedback on how customers engage with our products and services. This enables us to identify areas for improvement, make informed decisions for future development, and enhance overall customer satisfaction.
Optimize business operations: Data collection assists us in optimizing internal processes and resource allocation. By analyzing patterns and trends, we can identify operational inefficiencies, streamline workflows, and allocate resources more effectively.
Conduct research and development: Collected data fuels research and development efforts. It helps us identify emerging market trends, customer preferences, and areas for innovation, guiding our future product and service offerings.
Ensure security and prevent fraud: Data collection allows us to implement robust security measures to protect our customers’ information. By monitoring and analyzing data patterns, we can detect and prevent fraudulent activities, safeguarding both our customers and our business.
Regarding data sharing, we want to assure you that we prioritize the privacy and security of our customers’ data. We do not share any collected data with external entities or third parties. Our commitment is to maintain the confidentiality of the data we collect, adhering to applicable laws and regulations to protect the privacy of our customers.
Among the types of Personal Data that Zaytech Corp collects, by itself or through third parties, there are: Cookies, Usage Data, Personal Information, email address and various types of Data.
Complete details on each type of Personal Data collected are provided in the dedicated sections of this privacy policy or by specific explanation texts displayed prior to the Data collection. If you should suggest to us a customer, provide us his/her email address. From time to time, we may send an invitation email.
Additionally, Personal Data may be freely provided by the User, or, in case of Usage Data, collected automatically when using Zaytech Corp applications.
Unless specified otherwise, all Data requested by Zaytech Corp is mandatory and failure to provide this Data may make it impossible for Zaytech Corp to provide its services. In cases where Zaytech Corp specifically states that some Data is not mandatory, Users are free not to communicate this Data without consequences to the availability or the functioning of the Service.
Users who are uncertain about which Personal Data is mandatory are welcome to contact the Owner.
Any use of Cookies – or of other tracking tools – by Zaytech Corp or by the owners of third-party services used by Zaytech Corp serves the purpose of providing the Service required by the User, in addition to any other purposes described in the present document and in the Cookie Policy, if available.
Users are responsible for any third-party Personal Data obtained, published or shared through Zaytech Corp and confirm that they have the third party’s consent to provide the Data to the Owner.

Legal basis of processing
The Owner may process Personal Data relating to Users if one of the following applies:
– Users have given their consent for one or more specific purposes. Retention time
Personal Data shall be processed and stored for as long as required by the purpose they have been collected for.
Therefore:
– Personal Data collected for purposes related to the performance of a contract between the Owner and the User shall be retained until such contract has been fully performed.
– Personal Data collected for the purposes of the Owner’s legitimate interests shall be retained as long as needed to fulfill such purposes. Users may find specific information regarding the legitimate interests pursued by the Owner within the relevant sections of this document or by contacting the Owner.
The Owner may be allowed to retain Personal Data for a longer period whenever the User has given consent to such processing, as long as such consent is not withdrawn. Furthermore, the Owner may be obliged to retain Personal Data for a longer period whenever required to do so for the performance of a legal obligation or upon order of an authority.
Once the retention period expires, Personal Data shall be deleted. Therefore, the right to access, the right to erasure, the right to rectification and the right to data portability cannot be enforced after expiration of the retention period.
