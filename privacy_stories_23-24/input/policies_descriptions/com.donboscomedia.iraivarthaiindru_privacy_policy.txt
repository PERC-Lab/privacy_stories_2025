Iraivarthai Indru II Privacy Policy
Thank you for choosing Iraivarthai Indru II, a daily mass reading app developed by
DB Media Tiruchy . This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect any information you may provide while using our app.
Information We Do Not Collect
Iraivarthai Indru II does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not request access to your contacts, location, camera, or any other personal data.
Data Storage
All data within Iraivarthai Indru II is stored locally on your device. We do not store or transfer any user data to external servers or third parties.
Third-Party Services
Iraivarthai Indru II does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information. Any external links provided within the app are for informational purposes only, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes by updating the “Last updated” date at the beginning of this policy.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at
donboscomedia@gmail.com
By using Iraivarthai Indru II, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy.
