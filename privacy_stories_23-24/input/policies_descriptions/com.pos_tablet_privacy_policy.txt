Privacy Statement
At Blue Jay POS Inc , we are committed to protect the privacy of your personal information you have entrusted to us. We will not sell or share customer information to outside marketers or with any non-affiliated person, company, or organization except in legally permitted circumstances.
We collect the following information
•	Individual name, business name, mailing address, email address and phone number
•	Billing Information such as credit card number, checking account number, billing address and shipping address
We use this information to
•	Fulfill your order
•	Send you an order confirmation
•	Send you requested product or service information
•	Respond to customer service requests
•	Administer your account
•	Send you marketing communications
•	Respond to your questions and concerns
We collect information from the following sources
•	Applications or other forms you summit to us
•	Information from a consumer report
•	From public records and market research
•	Information we have obtained at your request
We will share information in the following circumstances
•	Comply with federal, state and local laws
•	Comply with credit reporting laws and card association rules.
•	Provide only necessary information to third parties in order to fulfill your requested services.
•	Provide only necessary information to third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf
Protecting your information
We authorize access to information about you for only those employees who need to know that information as part of their job responsibilities. We also educate our employees about the importance of confidentiality and customer privacy through standard operating procedures, special training programs, and our Code of Conduct. We take appropriate disciplinary measures to enforce employee privacy responsibilities. We also maintain strict information security procedures, including physical, electronic and procedural safeguards, to protect the confidentiality of your information. We will continue to test and update our technology to improve the protection of your information.
Changes to this statement
We will occasionally update this Statement of Privacy to reflect company and customer feedback. We encourage you to periodically review this Statement to be informed of
any changes to our Statement of Privacy.
